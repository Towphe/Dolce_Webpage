OUR STORY

Our company originated with a group of Pasig City Science High School students' decision to sell sweet, luscious graham balls and other authentic Filipino snacks at a low price. Having spent the past two years in lockdown, many people sought to look for healthier alternatives to satiate their appetite. Thus, we came up with more ways to satisfy cravings, and this time, we aim to do so in a healthy way!

WHAT'S SPECIAL ABOUT DOLCE?

Dolce has variety, healthy, affordable, customized puds. We at Dolce will help you satisfy your cravings with our affordable, diverse, customer-oriented and healthy sweets. (USP)

Our products help everyone, particularly people who want their sweet cravings satisfied without compromising their health. Our business can provide healthier, diverse, affordable, and high quality desserts, unlike other competitors. (VP)

Mission

We at Dolce aim to serve you the sweetest, most fulfilling snacks that will surely satisfy your cravings—one confection at a time!

Vision

Our goal is to sell delicious confectionery—with a twist! The snacks that we offer are not only homemade with love, they also contain healthy ingredients such as Stevia. Ultimately, we at Dolce aim to establish ourselves as the one-stop shop for anyone who's looking for nutritious, healthy snacks that people will enjoy nonetheless.
